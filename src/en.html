I'm lecturer

Hi, I'm Mild or Thada Wangthammang. I live in Hatyai, Songkhla, Thailand.

I'm currently lecturer at Prince of Songkla University International College (PSUIC), Digital Media Program. I'm enthusiastic to learn about and conduct some research in the areas of cloud technology and distributed and parallel computing, especially, in the area of web technology and game development.

I also love to be healthy. Running is my currently exercise. Another happiness is photography
